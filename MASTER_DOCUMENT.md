## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
DinApp is a phone-number-based financial marketplace platform delivering wallets, QR payments, creator monetization (gifts + subscriptions), a blind-bidding borrow/lend marketplace, and agent cash-outâ€”without banking or lending balance-sheet risk. Built sandbox-first, DinApp emphasizes auditability, AML/KYC controls, and capital-light scalability.

---

## 2. PRODUCT OVERVIEW & VISION
*   **Identity:** Phone number (E.164) + OTP + PIN
*   **Audience:** Unbanked, creators, informal vendors, SMEs
*   **Positioning:** Platform infrastructure (not a bank, not a lender)

---

## 3. MARKET PROBLEM & OPPORTUNITY
*   Cash-heavy informal economies
*   Limited creator cash-out options
*   Unsafe, opaque lending practices

*DinApp digitizes flow, not risk.*

---

## 4. PLATFORM ARCHITECTURE
*   Mobile (Flutter), backend microservices
*   Double-entry immutable ledger
*   mTLS inter-service security
*   Audit & compliance layer

---

## 5. CORE FEATURES (SUMMARY)
*   **Wallets:** Personal, Creator, Merchant, Agent, Escrow, Platform
*   **QR Scan-to-Pay:** OTP+PIN, receipts, audible confirmation
*   **Livestreaming & Gifting:** 55% Creator / 45% Platform
*   **Monthly Subscriptions (3 Tiers):** 55% Creator / 45% Platform
*   **Borrow/Lend:** Borrowers free; lenders pay 10% monthly on interest only; blind bidding
*   **Agents:** Accredited/sub-licensed cash-out with float wallets
